# LinkedIn Series: Building Brevia

**Series title:** Building Brevia — a URL shortener from scratch

**Audience:** Non-technical or lightly technical connections. Hiring managers, early-career folks, curious people.

**Goal:** Show that I think like an engineer — not just that I can code. Demonstrate planning, decision-making, and learning in public.

**Tone:** Casual, honest, short. No jargon without a quick explanation. First person.

## Recurring structure per post

- **Hook** (1–2 lines) — a question, a surprising fact, or a small story
- **The thing** (2–4 short paragraphs) — the idea, the decision, the lesson
- **Takeaway** (1 line) — what I learned or what it means
- **CTA** (1 line) — soft, not salesy ("curious what you'd have chosen?")

Each post should be under ~200 words. No headers in the actual LinkedIn posts — just short paragraphs with line breaks.
